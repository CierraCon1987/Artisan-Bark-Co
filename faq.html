Frequently Asked Questions

We offer Milk, Dark, and White chocolate for our Artisan Bark. Each type can be customized with a variety of toppings to suit your taste.

To maintain freshness, store your chocolate bark in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Avoid storing it in the refrigerator to prevent condensation and flavor changes.

Our chocolate bark has a shelf life of up to 6 months when stored properly in a cool, dry place.
